Dash4Teachers
Teachers call parents all the time, and after hanging up the phone, these calls are logged in a notebook or binder to help teachers remember what the call was about (especially when you have 150 kids). You’re probably thinking, “How antiquated!” And that’s why we designed Dash4Teachers, which was an iPhone app that allowe teachers to call and record conversations without the hassle of switching between phone and paper. Dash4Teachers was made for teachers to not only record conversations, but also for teachers to track which numbers are best to use as well as the best times to call.
I joined the team as the web designer and developer during the summer between my first and second years of teaching.
