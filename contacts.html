Contacts | Mobile Concept
While playing around on my phone, I thought, "There has to be a more engaging way of displaying and interacting with my contacts book." So here it is. My concept for the contacts book.
Instead of lists of names, I thought that it would be more engaging to have pictures of the contacts. These pictures can be pooled from web profiles, such as Facebook or Instagram. Rather than clicking into a contact in order to text or call the person, I also thought that a faster way of finding the function that you want to use would be to have the function pop up when your finger is on the contact’s picture. Much more intuitive.
The mockup was made in After Effects.
