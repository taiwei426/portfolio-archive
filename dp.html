The Daily Pennsylvanian
I was the design editor of The Daily Pennsylvania, the student-run school newspaper at the University of Pennsylvania.
Everyday I spent around 8 hours in the office designing the next day’s paper. I was in charge of laying out the newspaper, but my favorite thing to do were graphics. I loved coming up with ways to represent information clearly and succinctly. I also really liked compositing images together to make one coherent picture. Some of my graphics are displayed below.
My team and I won several Gold Circle Awards from the Columbia Scholastic Press Association, including Informational Graphics Portfolio, Page One Design Portfolio, and Feature Page Design Portfolio.
