Philadelphia City Planning Commission
I was hired by the Philadelphia City Planning Commission as an intern to design Philadelphia's comphrehensive plan. I did the initial drafts of the 250-page book before I graduated from college. I selected the color theme and designed the logo along with most of the graphics within the book.
To clearly show the three sections within the book, purple, orange and green were selected. Within each section, graphic elements were used to represent the subsections. Colors and graphics were designed by moi.
