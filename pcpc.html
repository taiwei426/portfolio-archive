Philadelphia City Planning Commission
I was hired by the Philadelphia City Planning Commission as an intern to design Philadelphia's comphrehensive plan. The Planing Commission had originally planned on hiring a full time designer, but that didn’t pan out the way they had hoped, so I got the privilege of designing most of the book till I graduated from college.
I did the initial drafts of the 250-page book. I selected the color theme, drafted the layout of the book, designed the logo along with most of the graphics within the book. To clearly show the three sections within the book, purple, orange and green were selected. Within each section, graphic elements were used to represent the subsections. Colors and graphics were designed by moi.
