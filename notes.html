Notes
Everyone writes memos in their Notes app, but I've always found that my memos are always really unorganized, so I came up with this concept where the notes are color coded. Each color is a category of notes, and you can tag your notes with the category's colors for easy sorting. Memos for work can be pink, for housekeeping can be purple, for random thoughts that pop into your brain throughout the day can be blue. Then later on, when you're looking for a particular note, you can sort through your app visually. You can sort blue, in which case you can see all the different thoughts that you've added into your Notes app. Or let's say you're at Target, then you can sort purple, and see all the various lists you may have created on what you need to buy.
