Housing Prices
Having just moved here to the Bay, I was blown away by how expensive houses are here, so here is my representation of median housing prices in the Bay.
The data represented in the graphic can be easily shown in a bar graph, but we've all seen enough boring bar graphs. Instead, I thought to let each of the bars follow a circular path. But what I especially like about the bars around the circle is that the viewer can quickly see what fraction of a $1 million the median housing prices are for each of the counties. In other words, the bars almost act as pie graphs. When a bar wraps 75% around the circle, you know that's 75% of a million, or $750,000.
